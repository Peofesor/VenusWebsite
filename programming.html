Implementation of Steering Behaviors for Realistic Flocking and Decision-Making

C++ & Vektoria Engine

Developed a modular NPC behavior system in C++ from the ground up. By combining force-based steering behaviors (flocking, wander, arrive) with a robust decision tree architecture, I created agents capable of both physically accurate movement and context-aware decision-making.

Single Agent: Seek, Flee, Arrive, and Kinematic/Dynamic Wander.

Group Dynamics (Flocking): Separation, Cohesion, and Alignment for swarm intelligence.
